# Roadmap and future features

This page provides an overview of Lawnchair's development status, future goals, and release philosophy.

### Development status

Lawnchair is an open-source project driven entirely by a community of volunteers. Our developers contribute in their free time, balancing the project with jobs, school, and other personal commitments.

Development activity can vary; there may be periods of rapid progress followed by quieter times. Rest assured, Lawnchair is still actively developed. You can always see the latest changes by checking the commit history on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/LawnchairLauncher/lawnchair) or by installing our Nightly builds.

### Release policy (ETAs)

We do not provide Estimated Times of Arrival (ETAs) for new releases. Given the volunteer nature of the project, setting deadlines is not feasible and creates unnecessary pressure. A new version will be released when it is ready.

### **Future goals and roadmap**

Our development is community-driven and depends on contributor availability, so specific timelines are not set. However, some of our high-level goals for future versions include:

* Porting all Lawnchair Legacy features to Lawnchair 2
* Add highly requested features from the community
* Improving the UX of Lawnchair to be simpler

For more detailed planning, you can review our project's roadmap and open issues on GitHub.

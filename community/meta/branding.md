# Branding

This page contains the official branding assets and visual guidelines for the Lawnchair project. Please adhere to these guidelines when representing the project.

### License

Unlike Lawnchair's source code, which is openly licensed under Apache License 2.0, the Lawnchair logo, wordmark, and all other official branding elements are **All Rights Reserved (ARR)**. This means they are proprietary and protected intellectual property.

Please follow the guidelines listed in the next section.

### Lawnchair brand guidelines

Please adhere to the following guidelines when representing the Lawnchair project:

You may use Lawnchair's branding for:

* **Accurate representation** when creating news articles, reviews, or educational content that accurately describes the Lawnchair project.
* **Community creations** that are non-commercial (e.g., fan-made wallpapers, device setups) that clearly do not imply official endorsement or creation by the Lawnchair team.

You may NOT use Lawnchair's branding for:

* **Commercial use**, such as products, services, or ventures.
* **Derivative products**, such as software, products, or services that use the Lawnchair name or logos.
* Using the assets in such a way that **implies endorsement** or affiliation with the project, its developers, or its partners.

Alongside this, you are NOT allowed to:

* modify, alter, or recolor the official logos or wordmarks without explicit permission, or
* use the assets in a misleading way.

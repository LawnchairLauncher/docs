# Translate Lawnchair

Help make Lawnchair accessible to more people around the world by contributing translations.

We use Crowdin to manage our translation efforts, making it easy for anyone to contribute, even without technical knowledge.

If you have any questions, it's best to ask our [community channels](community-channels.md) on Telegram or Discord, as the chat on Crowdin is not actively monitored.

# Crashes

Experiencing an app crash can be frustrating and disruptive. This guide will help you understand what to do when Lawnchair crashes and how to report it to our development team so we can investigate.

### How Lawnchair handles crash reports

Lawnchair includes an automatic crash reporting system. When the app crashes, you will typically receive a notification from the app. This notification allows you to quickly generate and share a unique link to the crash log, which is invaluable for diagnosis.

{% hint style="warning" %}
On Android 14 and newer, you must have **notifications** turned on for Lawnchair to receive these crash reports.
{% endhint %}

### How to capture and report a crash

1. When you see the crash notification from Lawnchair, tap **Upload file**.
2. A unique link to the crash log will be generated. Tap **Copy to clipboard**.
3. Report the crash on GitHub:
   1. Navigate to our [GitHub bug report form](https://github.com/LawnchairLauncher/lawnchair/issues/new?template=bug_report.yaml).
   2. Follow the steps listed in the screen.
      1. In the "Additional Information" section of the form, paste the crash log link you copied in step 2.
   3. Submit the form

### Basic troubleshooting steps for frequent crashes

If Lawnchair is crashing frequently, try these general steps:

<details>

<summary>Restart Lawnchair</summary>

1. Open **Home settings**.
2. Tap **More** <i class="fa-ellipsis-vertical">:ellipsis-vertical:</i>.
3. Tap **Restart Lawnchair**.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Clear application cache</summary>

1. Open **Home settings**.
2. Tap **More** <i class="fa-ellipsis-vertical">:ellipsis-vertical:</i> .
3. Tap **App info**.
4. On the screen that appears, tap **Clear cache**.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Restart your device</summary>

1. On most phones, press your phone's power button for about 30 seconds, or until your phone restarts.
2. On the screen, you might need to tap Restart <i class="fa-repeat">:repeat:</i>.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Consider resetting Lawnchair to its default settings</summary>

**Important:** Resetting Lawnchair will remove all your settings to defaults. Before performing this, back up your settings.

*

</details>
